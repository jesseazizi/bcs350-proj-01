Services

FSCDrive consists of various different components that work in tandem with each other to make educational cloud storage accessible and efficient.

1

☁️ Cloud file storage

With FSCDrive, your files are stored safely on the cloud. They can be accessed anywhere, anytime, on any device.

2

🤝 Collaboration

FSCDrive lets you easily share documents with other students to collaborate on projects and group tasks together. When you start a group project and class, you and your teammates are automatically given access to a group folder, which you can use to share and collaborate on group-related documents.

3

🗂️ Organization

FSCDrive automatically organizes your files into folders depending on semester, class, and module. Your class syllabus is used to generate folders for each module, assignment, and subject. With automatic file organization, you can spend less time sorting folders and more time getting the work that matters done.
